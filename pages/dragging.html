This is the sixth assignment. The task was to make a mini-game which utilizes drag and drop events.

Carnivores and Herbivores (Desktop only)

In this mini-game you have to categorize animals according to trophic criteria.
Collect animals of the same category in one place (click on them so they are all standing upright) and at the same time make sure that herbivores are not dangerously close to carnivores.
When you complete the task, the animals will give you a sign.
